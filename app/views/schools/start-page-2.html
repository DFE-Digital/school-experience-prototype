Manage school experience

Use this service if you're a primary or secondary school in England and want to offer and organise school experience for the current academic year.

The service will send you requests from people who want to become teachers.

This service is only for managing school experience in England.

Start now

Before you start

To help you decide whether to book them on an experience, you’ll be provided with their:

  • full name and contact details - including address, email and telephone number
  • individual requirements - including any accessibility requirements and preferred experience length, dates and subjects
  • teaching experience and motivations - including how far they've got towards becoming a teacher and why they want to teach

You'll be told if they’ve undergone a Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check. These details will not be verified by DfE.

It's your responsibility to decide if they comply with your individual school safeguarding policies and whether to accept their request.

To help cover costs, you can charge fees for school experience. You’ll need to sort out payment direct between yourselves and the individual candidate.

If you’d like to manage your school experience through this service, you’ll need to provide various placement details including:

  • your available placement dates and subjects
  • your DBS-check requirements
  • whether you charge a fee for school experience
