New management tool

We’ve updated the management tool on the service so you can now:

  • manage requests - including accepting and rejecting requests with automatic email confirmations sent to candidates
  • manage bookings - including cancelling bookings with automatic email confirmations sent to candidates
  • confirm candidate attendance - so you can help us reduce the number of candidate no-shows
  • manage dates - including adding, editing and disabling fixed dates for candidates
  • update school profiles - including adding, editing and removing school profile details
