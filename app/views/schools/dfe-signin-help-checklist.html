DfE Sign-in checklist

Before signing in to the service using DfE Sign-in for the first time or if you want to add manage school experience for another school - check the following:

  • you have a DfE Sign-in account
  • your DfE Sign-in account is associated with the school where you want to manage school experience requests and bookings
  • you have a 'School Experience Administrator' role

If any of the above do not apply:

  • contact your organisation's 'DfE Sign-in approver' (often your head or deputy head) to see if they can help
  • submit a DfE Sign-in support request
