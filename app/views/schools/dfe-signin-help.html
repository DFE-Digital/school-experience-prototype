DfE Sign-in help

You need a DfE Sign-in account username and password to manage school experience for an organisation.

! Warning These are not the same as your old School Experience Portal (SEP) login details and do not include your URN.

Don't worry if you don't have or don't know your details - all you need to do is contact your organisation's DfE Sign-in approver.

Your approver can help you:

  • set up a new account - if you don't already have one
  • sign in to your existing account - if you already have one
  • set up your account to make sure you can manage school experience for the right organisation

How do I to find my DfE Sign-in approver?

If you work in a school, your approver will usually be your head or deputy head.

If they're not - don't worry.
