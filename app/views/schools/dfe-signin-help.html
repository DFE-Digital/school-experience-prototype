DfE Sign-in help

You need a DfE Sign-in account username and password to manage school experience for any organisation or school.

! Warning These are not the same as your old School Experience Portal (SEP) login details and do not include your URN.

Don't worry if you don't have or don't know your details - all you need to do is contact your organisation's DfE Sign-in approver.

Your approver will be able to:

  • send you your new or existing username and password
  • make sure you can manage school experience for the right place
  • set you up with a 'School Experience Administrator' role

I want to find my DfE Sign-in approver

If you work in a school, your 'approver' will usually be your head or deputy head.

If they're not your approver - don't worry. They should know who it is and be able to put you in touch.

I cannot find or contact my DfE Sign-in approver
