Manage school experience

Use this service if you're a primary or secondary school in England and want to offer school experience to potential teachers.

You'll see details about candidates who've requested to visit your school so you can decide whether to offer them experience.

The service is currently only open to specific schools. To sign up email us with your URN at organise.school-experience@education.gov.uk.

You'll be asked to sign in using DfE Sign-in. Before you start check you're able to use DfE Sign-in to use this service.

OR

If you have not signed in to this service before or want add a school to manage its school experience make sure you read our DfE Sign-in help.

Start now

Before you start

The service will ask you to outline the kind of experience you offer including details about DBS-checks and any fees you charge candidates.

It's your responsibility to decide if candidates comply with your school DBS and safeguarding policies.
