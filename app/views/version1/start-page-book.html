Get a school experience placement

Find and book a school experience placement with a primary or secondary school in England.

To get school experience you must be:

  • at least 18 years of age and thinking about becoming a teacher
  • have or be taking a degree or equivalent overseas qualification
  • applying for teacher training within the upcoming academic year

This service is only available for school experience placements in England

Start now

Before you start

To help cover costs, some schools charge a fee for school experience placements. You’ll need to pay any fees direct to the individual schools.

You’ll need to provide:

  • personal details - including your address
  • contact details - including your email and telephone number

Some schools also require you to undergo a Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check.

This is a safeguarding check to confirm that you’re suitable and able to work with vulnerable groups such as children.

You’ll get a confirmation email after you’ve booked your school experience placement.
