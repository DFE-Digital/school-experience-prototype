Find and book a school experience placement

You should consider taking part in school experience so you can get a taste of what it’s like to be a teacher

Hundreds of primary and secondary school in England have school experience placements on offer across a range of subjects

There are 3 ways to find and book a school experience placement:

  • Arrange an independent placement - Arrange your own placement directly with a school by checking their website or contacting them for details.

    Find a suitable school using the Get information about schools

     

  • Arrange a placement through the Department for Education (DfE) Get a school experience placement service

    You must be applying for teacher training within the upcoming academic year to be eligible for these kinds of placements.

     

  • Ask someone you know - Talk to friends, family and people you meet.

Ask them if they can put you in touch with someone who can help you arrange a placement through a local school.
