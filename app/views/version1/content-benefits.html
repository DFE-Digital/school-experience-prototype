School experience: the benefits

School experience placements provide people interested in becoming teachers with a valuable insight into what teaching involves on a day-to-day basis.

They’re an excellent way for anyone with the right qualifications who’s thinking about becoming a teacher to find out what working in a classroom is really like.

They also offer teacher training candidates the opportunity to demonstrate their experience of working with children and young people as part of their application.

If you’re a teacher training candidate, you’ll be tested on your knowledge of the national curriculum for your subject and general teaching methods.

So, if you’re looking to enter initial teacher training (ITT), a good quality school experience placement can help you evidence this as well as your understanding of:

  • the role of a teacher
  • creative and innovative teaching styles
  • how to tailor lessons to cater for students with different abilities and backgrounds
