School experience: background and history

School experience was introduced as an educational programme for secondary schools in September 2011.

Since September 2012, school experience has been administered online through a central database and in September 2015 it was expanded to offer primary school placements.

The programme and the path towards becoming a teacher are supported by the Department for Education’s Get Into Teaching website which offers free support and advice services to anyone who’s interested in taking up teaching as a career.
