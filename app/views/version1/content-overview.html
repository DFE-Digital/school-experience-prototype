School experience: overview

School experience is the educational programme allowing people interested in becoming teachers to find out more about what working and teaching in a school is really like.

It provides anyone interested in teaching a first-hand taste of school life by offering in-school placements across the course of the academic year.

Undertaking a school placement isn’t a mandatory requirement but allows teacher training candidates to:

  • find out more about what’s involved in teaching a class
  • ask real teachers questions about what the profession is really like
  • show they’ve worked with children and young people as part of their teacher training application

All kinds of primary and secondary schools across England offer placements across a broad range of subjects providing a valuable insight into the teaching profession at all levels.
