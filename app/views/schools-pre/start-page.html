Organise school experience placements

Use this service if you're a primary or secondary school in England and want to offer and manage school experience placements for the current academic year.

The service will send you requests from people looking into becoming a teacher and who want to find out what it's like to work as one.

To help you decide whether to accept an individual candidate and book them on a placement, you'll be provided with their:

  • full name and contact details - including address, email and telephone number
  • placement requirements - including preferred placement length, dates and subjects
  • teaching experience and motivations - including how far they've got towards becoming a teacher and why they want to teach

You'll also be told if candidates have undergone a Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check. These details will not be verified by DfE.

It's your responsibility to decide if a candidate complies with your individual school safeguarding policies and whether to accept their placement request.

To help cover costs, you can charge fees for placements. You’ll need to sort out payment direct between yourselves and the individual candidate.

This service is only for organising school experience placements in England.

Before you start

If you’d like to manage your school experience through this service, you’ll need to provide various placement details including:

  • your DBS-check requirements
  • whether you charge a fee for placements
