How the service works

After you’ve requested a school experience placement, schools will contact you to discuss your placement request.

Be aware, during term time it could typically take up to 2 weeks for them to get in touch.

The school will email or call you to discuss your request and placement date and also use this contact to:

  • discuss your suitability for a placement at their site
  • offer and arrange the best placement date they have available
  • find out more about your placement requirements - including any disability or access needs
  • let you know about any background and security checks you might need to go through
  • let you know about any fees you might have to pay as part of your placement - including any related placement, parking or other fees

To help cover costs, some schools charge a fee for placements. You’ll need to pay any fees direct to the individual schools.

You’ll receive emails after you’ve requested a placement and to confirm when a school has booked you on a placement.
