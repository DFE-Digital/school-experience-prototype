Get school experience

Use this service if you're interested in becoming a teacher and want to find and request school experience in schools across England.

School experience allows you to find out more about teaching by visiting schools.

This service is only available for school experience at primary and secondary schools in England.

Start now

Before you start

To request school experience, you’ll need to provide:

  • personal details - including your address
  • contact details - including your email and telephone number

As part of making a school experience request, your details will be registered with DfE’s Get Into Teaching service.

This means you can contact the service at any time to get:

  • extra help finding school experience should you need it
  • tailored advice and information about becoming a teacher
