Get a school experience placement

Use this service to find and request a school experience placement with a primary or secondary school in England.

Individual schools will decide whether to offer you a placement.

Some will require you to undergo a Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check.

This is a safeguarding check to confirm you’re suitable and able to work with vulnerable groups such as children.

To help cover costs, some schools also charge a fee for placements. You’ll need to pay any fees direct to the individual schools.

This service is only available for school experience placements in England.

Before you start

If you’d like to request a school experience placement, you’ll need to provide:

  • personal details - including your address
  • contact details - including your email and telephone number

You’ll get a confirmation email after you’ve requested a school experience placement.
